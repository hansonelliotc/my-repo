Planetoids

Coming Soon

Planetoids is a competitive print-and-play board game for 2-4 players. Players vie for dominance over the precious crystal deposits without exhausting their resources too quickly. Planetoids has an abundance of strategic depth while still being approachable with a simple ruleset. Will you play a game of risky dice rolls with big rewards, or will you take a slower, more calculated path to victory? The choice is yours.

It will be available for purchase on PNPArcade and itch.io.

Artist: Pierce Richards (portfolio)
